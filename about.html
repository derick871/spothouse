What is it

Spothouse is a modern platform design to enable tenants find
the perfect home while relocating from one town to another.
We connect the tenant and the landlord in a secure way.


Objective

The main aim of the project is to simplify
the process of searching house by providing accurate location,
house standard and price all in one place.


What we offer

  • House location with map
  • Standard of the house
  • Direct communication with Landload
  • Clear monthly payment
